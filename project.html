Binary Network (MLM) System

Binary Network MLM system is our latest self-developed system. Before going to try the Binary MLM Software and its demo, you must get an idea, How binary plan work. Below, we explain the concept of Binary Compensation Plan and its commission's calculation. Binary MLM Plan is the simplest and one of the most successful MLM plans in the MLM industry. As the name binary indicates, Binary plan is specified on number 2. Every member is restricted to add more than two members in the first level or business center of the binary plan, But, there is no limitation on the depth of the network that a distributor can develop.

How does Commission Calculation Work?

In binary plan, commission calculations are carried out based on business volume points, not levels. A formula, which calculates the business volume in the left leg with the right leg is used here to calculate the commission here. The payable commission is equivalent to the lesser percentage of the sales volume leg, Known as weak-leg. For example, if left leg earns 500 points and the right 1000 points. Then, the commission is calculated as 10% of the left leg. that is, 10% of 500. And right leg will have a carry point for next the commission. Some companies introduced a specific point value system, which follows different carry forward rules to calculate the commission. The eerpayment is regulated using ceiling (Limiting the daily/weekly Business Volume or Sales Volume ).
